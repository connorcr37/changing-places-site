Consign With Us

Getting Started

Thinking about consigning? It's easy! Text photos of your gently used furniture or home decor to 515-777-8992, email us at jen@shopchangingplaces.com, or use the form below. We'll review your items, let you know what we can accept, and schedule a drop-off during store hours. Please make sure all items are clean, in excellent condition, and free of stains, pet hair, or damage.

Terms & Payouts

Consigned items are displayed in our store for up to four months. For items priced over $60, we apply a 15% discount every 30 days to help them sell (fun fact: 90% of our inventory sells in the first 30 days!). If your items haven't sold by the end of the four months, you can choose to restart the consignment period or arrange for pickup. If we don't hear from you, the items will be transferred to a store account and may be further discounted or donated.

You'll receive 45% of the final sale price. Payouts are issued on the 5th of the month following the sale. Payments under $100 are made in cash; payments over $100 are paid by check.

Pickup & Delivery

Need help moving items? We're happy to recommend a trusted full-service mover for a seamless experience.

If you're looking for a more budget-friendly option, we also offer store-arranged garage or curbside pickup and delivery on Wednesdays between 10 AM and 2 PM for a flat fee of $100 (within a 20-mile radius). This service is available for both consignors and buyers. Please note: our team is only able to pick up or drop off items that are ready and accessible in a garage or driveway. For insurance reasons, we're unable to enter homes or assist with moving items inside.

About Changing Places

Since opening in September 2008, Changing Places has been a trusted part of the Des Moines community—helping people buy and sell quality home furnishings with personality and purpose. Originally founded by Bill and Terrill Johnson, the shop later passed to Steph Martin, and is now lovingly run by Jen Denham.

What hasn't changed? A love for design, a commitment to sustainability, and the belief that every piece deserves a second story. Whether you're furnishing a new space, refreshing a room, or letting go of something you've loved, we make the process simple, stylish, and satisfying.

From vintage treasures to modern staples, our ever-changing inventory is handpicked for quality and charm. Come discover something special—or let us help you pass it on.
