Consign With Us!

How to Get Started

Thinking about consigning with us? It’s easy! Just send photos of your gently used furniture or home decor to 515-777-8992 or jen@shopchangingplaces.com. After we review your items, we’ll let you know what we can accept and help you schedule a drop-off during store hours. Please ensure all items are clean and in excellent condition—free of stains, pet hair, or damage.

Terms & Payouts

Consigned items are displayed in our store for up to four months. For items priced over $60, we apply a 15% discount every 30 days to help them sell (fun fact: 90% of our inventory sells in the first 30 days!). If your items haven't sold by the end of the four months, you can choose to restart the consignment period or arrange for pickup. If we don’t hear from you, the items will be transferred to a store account and may be further discounted or donated.

You’ll receive 45% of the final sale price. Payouts are issued on the 5th of the month following the sale. Payments under $100 are made in cash, and those over $100 are paid by check.

Pickup & Delivery

Need help moving items? We’re happy to recommend a trusted full-service mover for a seamless, all-in-one experience.

If you're looking for a more budget-friendly option, we also offer store-arranged garage or curbside pickup and delivery on Wednesdays between 10 AM and 2 PM for a flat fee of $100 (within a 20-mile radius). This service is available for both consignors and buyers. Please note: our team is only able to pick up or drop off items that are ready and accessible in a garage or driveway. For insurance reasons, we’re unable to enter homes or assist with moving items from inside.

About Changing Places

Since opening its doors in September 2008, Changing Places has been a trusted part of the community—helping people buy and sell quality home furnishings with character. Originally founded by Bill and Terrill Johnson, the shop was later owned by Steph Martin and is now thoughtfully and passionately run by Jen Denham.

What’s stayed the same over the years? A love for design, a commitment to sustainability, and a belief that every piece deserves a second story. Whether you're furnishing a new space, refreshing a room, or rehoming something you’ve loved, we’re here to make the process simple, stylish, and rewarding.

From vintage treasures to modern staples, our ever-changing inventory is handpicked for quality and charm. Come find something special—or let us help you pass it on.
